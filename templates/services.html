Flake Flooring Systems

Durable, decorative flake systems combining beauty, texture, and exceptional slip resistance for high-traffic areas.

Vinyl flake flooring offers the perfect blend of aesthetics and functionality. Colored flakes are broadcast into the epoxy base coat, then sealed with a clear topcoat for a textured, attractive finish that hides imperfections and provides superior traction.

Key Features:

  • Enhanced slip resistance for safety
  • Hides floor imperfections and stains
  • Wide variety of flake colors and blends
  • Extremely durable and impact-resistant
  • Ideal for garages, basements, and workshops
  • Chemical and stain resistant
