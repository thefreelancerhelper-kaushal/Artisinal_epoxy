About Nova Scotia Epoxy Flooring

Your trusted partner for premium epoxy flooring solutions since 2019

Our Story

Established in 2019, Nova Scotia Epoxy Flooring was founded on a simple principle: deliver exceptional quality and craftsmanship that stands the test of time.

What started as a passion for transforming ordinary concrete into stunning, durable surfaces has grown into Nova Scotia's premier epoxy flooring company. We've completed hundreds of residential and commercial projects, each one reflecting our commitment to excellence.

We believe that your floors deserve the best. That's why we exclusively use premium Soprema epoxy systems paired with ALSAN pigments to create finishes that are not only beautiful but built to last decades.

Premium Materials, Outstanding Results

We use only the finest products in the industry

Soprema Epoxy Systems

We exclusively use Soprema, a world-renowned manufacturer of high-performance epoxy coatings. Their advanced formulations provide superior adhesion, durability, and chemical resistance that outperforms standard epoxy products.

ALSAN Pigments

Our metallic and custom color finishes feature premium ALSAN pigments known for their vibrant, consistent colors and UV stability. These professional-grade pigments create stunning visual effects that won't fade over time.

Our Specializations

Metallic Epoxy Systems

Creating unique, three-dimensional metallic effects that transform floors into works of art. Each installation is one-of-a-kind with swirling patterns and depth.

Flake Flooring

Decorative vinyl flake systems offering texture, slip resistance, and visual appeal. Perfect for garages, basements, and commercial spaces needing both beauty and function.

Residential Projects

Garages, basements, kitchens, and living spaces transformed with custom epoxy solutions tailored to your home's aesthetic and functional needs.

Commercial Flooring

Industrial-strength coatings for retail, warehouses, manufacturing facilities, and showrooms. Designed to withstand heavy traffic and demanding environments.
